### Primary Fire

**Homing crescent shots.** Two crescent-shaped projectiles fired from the wings, each homing toward the nearest enemy. Costs 1 mana per fire. Teal glow (#00CED1). ~2.7 damage per crescent, 0.35 s cooldown.

### Secondary Fire

**Charged shot.** Hold to charge a ball of energy in front of the dragon; release to fire. Mana drains at 4/s while charging. Damage scales with charge time (0.2 s min to 2 s max). Red/gold/teal gradient. Faster projectile (280 px/s) on release.

### Shield

**Meditating.** Red sprite tracing with fire-like effect (orange/red gradient). No damage reduction. Increases mana regen 1.75× (4/s → 7/s). Cannot fire while meditating. Free—pure sustain mode.
