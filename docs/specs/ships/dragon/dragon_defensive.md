## Ship

Dragon is the mana archetype—high mana pool, fast regen, mana-centric weapons. Multiple weapon ports, ornate gunship. Orange/red (#FF4500) propulsion. High Mana (28), lower HP/Defense (18/16). Glass cannon, mana ship identity.

## Pilot

Defensive specialist. Zen, brave, immovable fortitude. Thicker build, older. More protection than others. Calm, steady. Brass/copper, heavier armor. "I hold the line."

## Pairing

**Flavor:** Contrast pairing. Dragon's glass cannon gets a shield. Still mana-focused, still dangerous—but survivable. The caster that can take a hit.

**Interaction:** Defensive pilot increases HP, defense; reduces speed. Dragon becomes less fragile. Mana ship with staying power.

### Primary Fire

**Homing crescent shots.** Two crescent-shaped projectiles fired from the wings, each homing toward the nearest enemy. Costs 1 mana per fire. Teal glow (#00CED1). ~2.7 damage per crescent, 0.35 s cooldown.

### Secondary Fire

**Charged shot.** Hold to charge a ball of energy in front of the dragon; release to fire. Mana drains at 4/s while charging. Damage scales with charge time (0.2 s min to 2 s max). Red/gold/teal gradient. Faster projectile (280 px/s) on release.

### Shield

**Meditating.** Red sprite tracing with fire-like effect (orange/red gradient). No damage reduction. Increases mana regen 1.75× (4/s → 7/s). Cannot fire while meditating. Free—pure sustain mode.
