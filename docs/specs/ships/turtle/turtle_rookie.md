## Ship

Turtle is the tank archetype—heavy, durable, armored. Thicker hull than Sparrow. Earth brown, gold, copper, brass. Amber/gold (#FFBF00) propulsion. High HP/Defense (26/24), low Speed (16). Hold position, absorb hits.

## Pairing

**Flavor:** Pure turtle. No pilot tweaks—just the ship. Reliable, armored, deliberate. The baseline tank experience.

**Interaction:** Rookie applies no modifiers. Turtle's base stats: high HP, high defense, low speed.

### Primary Fire

**Arc shot.** Curved beam that shoots out in front of the ship. Static arc, ~4 damage, 0.25 s cooldown. Amber (#FFBF00). 0.2 s duration. No mana cost.

### Secondary Fire

**Spread shot.** 8 round projectiles in all directions (0°, 45°, … 315°). 5 mana, 1.5 s cooldown. ~2 damage per projectile. 180 px/s. Amber palette.

### Shield

**Fortress barrier.** Force-field zone; other ships can enter to be shielded. 65% damage reduction. 0.75 mana/s drain. Configurable radius (~150–180 px).
