## Ship

Sparrow is the fast archetype—sleek, nimble, bird-inspired. Smallest of the four ships. Cobalt/cyan hull, aether blue (#00FFFF) propulsion glow. High Speed (35), low HP/Defense (14/12). Evasion over tanking.

## Pilot

Defensive specialist. Zen, brave, immovable fortitude. Thicker build, older. More protection than others. Calm, steady. Brass/copper, heavier armor. "I hold the line."

## Pairing

**Flavor:** Contrast pairing. Sparrow's fragility softened by Defensive pilot—still nimble, but survivable. The fast ship that can take a hit.

**Interaction:** Defensive pilot increases HP, defense; reduces speed. Sparrow becomes less extreme, more forgiving. Balanced Sparrow.

### Primary Fire

**Basic gun.** Straight projectile from center nose. 5 damage per shot, 0.15 s cooldown (~6.67/s). Cyan (#00FFFF). 240 px/s projectile speed.

### Secondary Fire

**Energy rings.** Elliptical rings that spawn at muzzle and grow as they travel. 1 mana per ring, 0.12 s fire rate. Cyan palette. Damage via weaponStrength.

### Shield

**Personal glow.** Full-circle shield around ship. 50% damage reduction. 1 mana/s drain while held.
