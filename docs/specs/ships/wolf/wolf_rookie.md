## Pairing

**Flavor:** Pure wolf. The baseline. No specialization, no weakness. Reliable, versatile, predictable.

**Interaction:** Rookie applies no modifiers. Wolf's balanced stats (all 20s) unchanged. Ideal for players who want a neutral starting point.

### Primary Fire

**Dual wing-tip shots.** Two projectiles from left and right wing tips, fired simultaneously. 2.5 damage per shot, 0.3 s cooldown. White/silver (#E8E8E8). 240 px/s.

### Secondary Fire

**Solid laser beam.** Hold-to-fire beam from center nose. 5 mana/s drain. 5 dps. Unlimited length while held. White/silver/grey. 14 px width, taper at back.

### Shield

**Front-half barrier.** Arc covering front semicircle only; back half exposed. 45% damage reduction (when attack from front). 0.8 mana/s. Deals 1 dps to enemies in contact.
