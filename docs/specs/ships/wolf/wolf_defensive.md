## Ship

Wolf is the balanced archetype—versatile, no specialization. Fighter jet silhouette. Grey, silver, brass. White/silver propulsion. All stats at 20. Jack-of-all-trades: decent evasion, durability, damage.

## Pilot

Defensive specialist. Zen, brave, immovable fortitude. Thicker build, older. More protection than others. Calm, steady. Brass/copper, heavier armor. "I hold the line."

## Pairing

**Flavor:** Balanced with bulk. Wolf gains Defensive pilot's toughness—the all-rounder that can absorb more. Steady, unshakeable.

**Interaction:** Defensive pilot increases HP, defense; reduces speed. Wolf becomes tankier without losing versatility. Balanced tank.

### Primary Fire

**Dual wing-tip shots.** Two projectiles from left and right wing tips, fired simultaneously. 2.5 damage per shot, 0.3 s cooldown. White/silver (#E8E8E8). 240 px/s.

### Secondary Fire

**Solid laser beam.** Hold-to-fire beam from center nose. 5 mana/s drain. 5 dps. Unlimited length while held. White/silver/grey. 14 px width, taper at back.

### Shield

**Front-half barrier.** Arc covering front semicircle only; back half exposed. 45% damage reduction (when attack from front). 0.8 mana/s. Deals 1 dps to enemies in contact.
