### Cross-cutting principles (from sophisticated refs)

- **Higher resolution preferred:** Crisp, sharp rendering; visible detail at native resolution; polished surfaces with reflections and highlights
- **Detailed sprites:** Shading, metallic textures, panel lines, volumetric appearance
- **Dynamic VFX:** Glowing projectiles, energy beams, explosions, particle trails
- **Layered backgrounds:** Parallax, depth, atmospheric elements (clouds, nebula, stars)
- **Rich color contrast:** Bright effects against darker backgrounds
- **Biomechanical enemies:** Organic + mechanical fusion; visible internal structures
- **Thematic UI:** Lore-integrated framing, textured backgrounds, ancient script overlays
- **Hit feedback:** Starburst explosions, damage indicators on enemies

## Takeaways (level design)

- **Terrain is primary:** Ocean, land, coastline, forest, mountains—not abstract sky
- **Top-down view of terrain:** Clear separation (water vs land), readable at a glance
- **Tiled/textured backgrounds:** Limited palette, high contrast for ships/projectiles
- **Environmental obstacles:** Land formations, structures, vertical barriers
