# Narrative Bible

## World Rules

- Earth has flying machines (Vimanas) capable of fighting back against the alien invasion
- Pilots are special chosen citizens destined to be Vimana pilots; more capable than average humans
- Vimanas resemble steampunk spaceships
- Victory requires teamwork; solo pilots cannot overcome the invasion

## The Big Bad

- Race of insect hive-mind aliens invading Earth
- Ancient civilization period setting
- Invaders approach from the Mother Ship
- Each level advances the story as pilots get closer to stopping the invasion at its source

## Factions

- **Humanity:** Pilots, chosen citizens, defenders of Earth
- **The Invaders:** Insect hive-mind aliens, Mother Ship as source

## Timeline

- Sequential levels; each level has a new story development
- Arc: pilots take off from base → fight waves → reach boss → progress toward Mother Ship

## Characters (4 Pilots)

- **Speed specialist:** hasty/impatient; makes ships faster, less defense
- **Weapon specialist:** loves blowing shit up, cowboy attitude; stronger/faster/bigger attacks
- **Defensive specialist:** zen, brave, immovable fortitude; tougher ships, slower
- **Neutral:** rookie with can-do attitude; no modifying effect on ships
