## Mission Briefing — Level 1: The Coastline

**Title:** First Contact

**Briefing:**

> The invaders came from the sky—not gods, not omens. *Things.* Hive-minded, relentless. They've been probing our coast for weeks. Today we push back.
>
> Your Vimanas are ready. The ancient craft—stone, bronze, alien alloy—have waited centuries for pilots like you. Clear the coastline. Break their formation. Find out what they're protecting.
>
> We don't know what's at the end of that swarm. But we know this: alone, we fall. Together, we fly.

**Objective:** Destroy all waves. Reach the boss. Survive.
