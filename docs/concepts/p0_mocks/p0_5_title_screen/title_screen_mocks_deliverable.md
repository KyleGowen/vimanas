## Design Intent

A title screen that immediately communicates: **steampunk flying machines**, **ancient civilization under siege**, and **cooperative action**. Polished enough for investor presentations. **Epic but achievable**—not grimdark; hopeful defiance. **Cooperative**—fleet of 1–4 ships suggests teamwork. Kaladesh aesthetic throughout.

## Visual Elements

### Title Treatment
- **"VIMANAS"** — large, bold. ONLY this word—no HERO, no subtitle, no extra text.
- Brass (#B5A642) / copper (#B87333) metallic gradient with warm highlights
- Engraved or embossed feel (Kaladesh ornate, not Victorian)
- Optional: subtle gear or wing motif integrated into letterforms

### Background
- Layered parallax-ready sky: deep twilight or dawn
- Clouds with warm (amber, copper) and cool (slate) tones
- Distant horizon suggesting ancient civilization (temple silhouettes, mountains)
- Optional: subtle insectoid shapes in clouds or sky—threat implied, not explicit

### Ships (Hero Asset)
- One of each ship (Sparrow, Turtle, Wolf, Dragon)—four distinct ships. Reference: [sparrow/sparrow_ship_kaladesh.png](../p0_1_ships/sparrow/sparrow_ship_kaladesh.png), [turtle/turtle_ship_kaladesh.png](../p0_1_ships/turtle/turtle_ship_kaladesh.png), [wolf/wolf_ship_kaladesh_v4.png](../p0_1_ships/wolf/wolf_ship_kaladesh_v4.png), [dragon/dragon_ship_kaladesh_v3.png](../p0_1_ships/dragon/dragon_ship_kaladesh_v3.png).
- **Propulsion glow colors (canonical):** Sparrow=cyan (#00FFFF), Turtle=amber (#FFBF00), Wolf=white/silver, Dragon=orange/red (#FF4500). See [art_style_guide.md](../../../art_style_guide.md).
- **Iteration 8:** Propulsion glow applied—each ship has colored engine trails; ships remain silhouettes.
- **Iteration 9:** Ships at middle depth (building level, among cityscape). Glow on various ship parts (wings, accents, cores, vents)—not just exhaust.
- **Iteration 10 (locked in):** Reference composition + glow effects from dynamic variant. Ships in sky above cityscape. Glow on wings, accents, cores, vents, trails. **CEO approved 2025-03-02.**
- All four face right; smaller scale; cast in silhouette (setting sun backlight); other airships in scene.

### Prompt Line
- **PRESS START • 1–4 PLAYERS** — platform-appropriate, clear, readable
- Copper or brass framing / period-appropriate typography

### Palette (from Art Style Guide)
- Brass (#B5A642), copper (#B87333), wood tones
- Cobalt/cyan accents (Sparrow hull)
- Warm metallics for title
- Dark slate/sky for contrast and readability
