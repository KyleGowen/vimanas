## Design Intent

A title screen that immediately communicates: **steampunk flying machines**, **ancient civilization under siege**, and **cooperative action**. Polished enough for investor presentations. **Style:** Illustrated, sleek—high-fidelity 2D, NOT pixel art or 16-bit. See [art_style_guide.md](../../art_style_guide.md).

## Visual Elements

### Title Treatment
- **"VIMANAS"** — large, bold, period-appropriate typography
- Brass/copper metallic gradient with warm highlights
- Slight engraved or embossed feel (steampunk)
- Optional: subtle gear or wing motif integrated into letterforms

### Background
- Layered parallax-ready sky: deep twilight or dawn
- Clouds with warm (amber, copper) and cool (slate) tones
- Distant horizon suggesting ancient civilization (temple silhouettes, mountains)
- Subtle insectoid shapes in clouds or sky — threat implied, not explicit

### Hero Asset
- Sparrow ship (or small fleet of 1–4 ships) in silhouette
- Positioned below or flanking the title
- Reads as "our heroes" — sleek, ready for action

### Palette (from Art Style Guide)
- Brass (#B5A642), copper (#B87333), wood tones
- Cobalt/cyan accents (Sparrow hull)
- Warm metallics for title
- Dark slate/sky for contrast and readability

---

## Tone

- **Epic but achievable** — not grimdark; hopeful defiance
- **Cooperative** — fleet of ships suggests teamwork
- **Steampunk meets ancient** — flying machines, not sci-fi rockets

---

## Implementation Notes

- Designed for 16:9 (Steam, Switch TV mode)
- Safe zones for title and prompt (overscan on TV)
- Animated: gentle cloud drift, subtle ship idle, title shimmer optional
- "PRESS START" or "PRESS ANY BUTTON" — platform-appropriate
