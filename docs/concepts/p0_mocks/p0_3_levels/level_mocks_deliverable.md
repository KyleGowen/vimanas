## Level 3: Sky

**Design intent:** High-altitude sky level—**strict overhead view** looking straight down through cloud layers. **Match forest and industrial mock perspective exactly**—camera directly above, bird's eye, map-like. Cloud tops as fluffy shapes seen from above (like looking down at cotton balls). Floating Kaladesh temple platforms, golden domes, jewel-like energy nodes. **NO airplanes. NO circles or decorative frames in corners. NO horizon line—strict top-down only.** Bright, optimistic; aether glow.

**Status: LOCKED IN (CEO approved 2025-03-02)**
