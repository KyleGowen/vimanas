# Player Ship POC: Turtle (Slow Archetype)

**Visual Design Agent · Phase 2 · MILESTONE_001 · 2025-03-01**

### Canonical design (starting point)

![Turtle ship](turtle_ship_kaladesh.png)  
*Turtle: Spaceship, just thicker. Earth brown, gold, copper, brass. Filigree, aether glow. Durable fighter.*

### Design rule

**Spaceship, thicker.** Looks like a spacecraft, not an animal. Heavier hull than Sparrow. No turtle/animal qualities.

---

## Design Intent

The Turtle is the tank—slow, tough, immovable. Shape communicates durability. Thicker hull than Sparrow; reads as "defense" at a glance. **No turtle/animal qualities**—it's a spaceship, just thicker.

---

## Silhouette (Top-Down Primary)

**Top-down view is paramount.** Ships are seen from above during gameplay.

- **Spaceship, thicker:** Same fighter style as Sparrow. Heavier hull. No shell, no animal features.
- **Top-down read:** Thicker, less aerodynamic than Sparrow. Heavy.
- **Size:** Slightly larger than Sparrow. Suggests durability.

---

## Vimana Language

- **Earth brown, gold, copper, brass:** Heavy, grounded. Ornate mechanical.
- **Thick hull:** Armored. Filigree, gears. Beauty alongside functionality.
- **Exhaust:** Slower; aether glow or subtle. Not flashy.
- **Detail:** Heavier than Sparrow. Ornate geometric patterns.
