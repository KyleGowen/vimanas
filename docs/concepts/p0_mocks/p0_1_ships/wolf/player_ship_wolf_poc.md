# Player Ship POC: Wolf (Neutral Archetype)

**Visual Design Agent · Phase 2 · MILESTONE_001 · 2025-03-01**

### Canonical design (starting point)

![Wolf ship](wolf_ship_kaladesh_v4.png)  
*Wolf: Fighter jet silhouette. Less busy, no wolf head. Grey, silver, gold, copper. Clean lines. Balanced.*

### Design rule

**Fighter-like, nimble.** Fighter jet silhouette. Less busy, no wolf head. Mid-size, balanced. Single compact spacecraft.

---

## Design Intent

The Wolf is the balanced ship—no specialization. Mid-size, neutral. Menacing front, cockpit in front. Reads as "reliable, versatile, threatening" at a glance.

---

## Silhouette (Top-Down Primary)

**Top-down view is paramount.** Ships are seen from above during gameplay.

- **Balanced:** Moderate wingspan. Fighter jet silhouette. Clean lines. Less busy.
- **Top-down read:** Between Sparrow and Turtle in size. Not sleek, not heavy.
- **Size:** Mid-size. Neutral.

---

## Vimana Language

- **Grey, silver, bronze, brass:** Neutral palette. Doesn't lean toward speed or defense.
- **Balanced detail:** Ornate panels. Copper, gold accents.
- **Moderate exhaust:** Aether glow or moderate. Not flashy.
