## Enemy Hierarchy

### Scout (Tier 1)
- **Size:** Smallest. Slightly smaller than Sparrow. Swarm-like.
- **Silhouette:** Insect-like—elongated body, wing-like appendages, angular. Reads as "alien" and "swarm" at a glance.
- **Detail:** Carapace-like hull; segmented. Sharp, angular forms—hard-edged exoskeleton, elongated bodies, mandible-like protrusions. Avoid bulbous or rounded.
- **Palette:** Amber (#FFBF00), olive-green (#6b8e23), dark brown (#3d2914). Warm, organic; contrasts with player's cool blue.
- **Behavior read:** Approaching from above; threat direction clear. V-formation or staggered wedge.
- **VFX:** Small explosion on death; amber/green projectiles.

### Medium (Tier 2)
- **Size:** 1.5–2× Scout. More imposing.
- **Silhouette:** Heavier body; more limbs or appendages. Larger wing span. Still insectoid.
- **Detail:** More armor segments; visible internal structures (glowing vents, energy cores). Biomechanical hints.
- **Palette:** Olive-green, purple-grey (#6b6b8e), dark brown. Slightly darker than Scout.
- **Behavior read:** Mid-tier threat. Can take more hits; may have secondary weapons.
- **VFX:** Larger explosion; possible hit feedback (starbursts on hull).

### Elite (Tier 3)
- **Size:** 2–3× Scout. Significant.
- **Silhouette:** Multi-segment; more limbs. Boss-like but smaller. Distinct from standard enemies.
- **Detail:** Biomechanical fusion—organic + mechanical. Tubes, ribs, glowing eyes. See sophisticated_ref_5.png.
- **Palette:** Purple-grey, dark brown, amber accents. More imposing.
- **Behavior read:** Mini-boss feel. Unique firing patterns; health bar.
- **VFX:** Starburst explosions; visible damage on hull.

### Boss (Tier 4)
- **Size:** Screen-filling or near. Dominant.
- **Silhouette:** Multi-limbed, multi-segment. Organic alien boss. See sophisticated_ref_1.png, sophisticated_ref_8.png.
- **Detail:** Biomechanical fusion. Gears, vents, radial modules. Visible internal structures. Health bar.
- **Palette:** Dark brown, purple-grey, amber/orange for glowing cores. Thematic.
- **Behavior read:** End-of-level threat. Unique patterns, phases.
- **VFX:** Large explosions; energy beams; shield effects.

## Ship Variant Notes (Turtle, Wolf, Dragon)

**Theme:** Ancient civilization + ancient aliens setting. Top-down view paramount.

**Design rule:** Fighter-like, nimble. NOT mother ships. Sparrow is the reference—all ships follow suit.

### Turtle (Slow Archetype)
- **Role:** Tank. Slower, tougher.
- **Silhouette:** Spaceship, just thicker. No turtle/animal qualities. Heavier hull.
- **Top-down:** Thicker than Sparrow. Less aerodynamic. No shell, no animal features.
- **Palette:** Earth brown (#4a3728), stone, bronze, copper (#b87333). Ancient, heavy.
- **Detail:** Armored plates. Thick hull. Bronze/copper accents.
- **Engine:** Slower, less visible exhaust. Maybe steam or subtle glow.
- **Size:** Slightly larger than Sparrow.

### Wolf (Neutral Archetype)
- **Role:** Balanced. No specialization.
- **Silhouette:** Mid-size, balanced. No wolf head—clean, minimal. Not busy.
- **Top-down:** Moderate wingspan. Snout-like nose cone. Not as sleek as Sparrow, not as heavy as Turtle.
- **Palette:** Grey (#808080), silver (#C0C0C0), bronze accents. Neutral.
- **Detail:** Standard panels. Balanced.
- **Engine:** Moderate exhaust. Balanced.
- **Size:** Between Sparrow and Turtle.

### Dragon (High Attack Archetype)
- **Role:** Lots of guns. Aggressive.
- **Silhouette:** Single-person fighter jet. Same scale as Sparrow—compact, NOT airliner. Multiple gun mounts. Normal wings.
- **Top-down:** Long, angular. Multiple weapon ports. Distinct "head" and "body."
- **Palette:** Dark red (#8B0000), copper, bronze. Hot, aggressive.
- **Detail:** Weapon ports, energy cores. Geometric patterns.
- **Engine:** Multiple exhausts; visible glow. Orange/red.
- **Size:** Same scale as Sparrow—compact. Most weapon ports of the four.
