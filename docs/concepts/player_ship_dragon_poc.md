# Player Ship POC: Dragon (High Attack Archetype)

**Visual Design Agent · Phase 2 · MILESTONE_001 · 2025-03-01**

### Canonical design (starting point)

![Dragon ship](dragon_ship_kaladesh_v3.png)  
*Dragon: NO dragon head. Aether-powered—glowing blue energy cores, luminous accents. Gold, copper, brass. Single-person fighter jet, compact.*

### Design rule

**Single-person fighter jet.** Same scale as Sparrow—small, nimble. NO dragon head. Aether-powered. Lots of guns but compact.

---

## Design Intent

The Dragon is the gunship—lots of weapons, aggressive. Largest of the four. Multiple gun mounts. Reads "firepower" at a glance. **Normal wings**—no dragon head on wings.

---

## Silhouette (Top-Down Primary)

**Top-down view is paramount.** Ships are seen from above during gameplay.

- **Single-person fighter jet:** Compact, same scale as Sparrow. NOT airliner, NOT big.
- **Gunship:** Multiple weapon ports. Normal-looking wings.
- **Top-down read:** Aggressive. Multiple exhausts. Small, nimble.

---

## Vimana Language

- **Dark red, copper, gold, brass:** Hot, aggressive palette. Ornate mechanical.
- **Weapon ports:** Multiple. Gun-focused. Normal wings. Aether energy cores—glowing blue. NO dragon head. Ornate detail.
- **Multiple exhausts:** Orange/red or aether glow. Visible. Beauty alongside firepower.
