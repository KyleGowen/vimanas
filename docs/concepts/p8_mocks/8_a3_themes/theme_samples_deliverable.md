# Theme Samples — 8.A.3 Deliverable (Level Theme Taxonomy)

**Phase 8 · 8.A.3**

Visual samples for each theme in the [Level Theme Taxonomy](../level_theme_taxonomy.md). One image per theme (forest, industrial, sky, city_metropolis, volcano). **Style:** Illustrated, sophisticated, Kaladesh-inspired. Strict top-down overhead. NOT pixel art. Rich detail, atmospheric.

---

## Forest

**Design intent:** Forest canopy from above—**strict overhead view**. Dense foliage, varied greens. **Kaladesh-inspired**—gilded temple or structure tops visible, ornate garden paths, jewel accents. Rich detail.

![Forest theme sample](theme_forest_sample.png)  
*Overhead view: tree crown tops, foliage patches, circular canopy shapes. Gilded structure tops, ornate paths, decorative elements. Parallax: far canopy/sky gaps, mid dense tree tops, near leaves/branches.*

### Parallax Layers

| Layer | Content | Scroll Ratio |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| Far | Distant canopy, sky gaps | 0.3 |
| Mid | Dense tree tops, varied greens | 0.6 |
| Near | Leaves, branches, ornate paths | 1.0 |

### Palette

#2d6a2d, #6b8e23, #8fbc8f, earth brown, #B5A642, #B87333

## Industrial

**Design intent:** Industrial area as seen from above—**strict overhead view**. Grey pipes, conduits, machinery tops, copper and brass. Kaladesh ornate accents on rooftops but INDUSTRIAL character—no forest, no greenery. Architecture only.

![Industrial theme sample](theme_industrial_sample.png)  
*Overhead view: building tops, conduit networks, machinery from above. Ornate rooftops, gilded conduits, temple-like structures. Parallax: far structures, mid ornate rooftops/conduits, near detail.*

### Parallax Layers

| Layer | Content | Scroll Ratio |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| Far | Distant structures, sky | 0.3 |
| Mid | Ornate rooftops, gilded conduits | 0.6 |
| Near | Conduit detail, machinery tops | 1.0 |

### Palette

#4a4a4a, #B87333, #B5A642, aether blue

## Sky

**Design intent:** High-altitude sky level—**strict overhead view** looking straight down through cloud layers. Cloud tops as fluffy shapes seen from above. Floating Kaladesh temple platforms, golden domes, jewel-like energy nodes. Bright, optimistic; aether glow.

![Sky theme sample](theme_sky_sample.png)  
*Overhead view: cloud tops, layered sky. Parallax: far deep sky, mid cloud banks, near wisps. Gilded accents, ornate temple/structure silhouettes, floating platforms.*

### Parallax Layers

| Layer | Content | Scroll Ratio |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| Far | Deep sky, horizon haze | 0.3 |
| Mid | Cloud banks, aether glow, gilded accents | 0.6 |
| Near | Cloud wisps, atmospheric detail, filigree | 1.0 |

### Palette

#87CEEB, amber, gold #B5A642, copper #B87333, white/soft grey

## City Metropolis

**Design intent:** Urban Kaladesh from above—**strict overhead view**. Dense metropolitan architecture, warm metallics, aether accents. Rooftops, streets, vertical structures. **Distinct from industrial**—more vertical, metropolitan, urban density.

![City Metropolis theme sample](theme_city_metropolis_sample.png)  
*Overhead view: rooftops, streets, building tops. Dense urban skyline. Parallax: far skyline/haze, mid rooftops/streets, near building detail.*

### Parallax Layers

| Layer | Content | Scroll Ratio |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| Far | Distant skyline, haze | 0.3 |
| Mid | Rooftops, streets, structures | 0.6 |
| Near | Building detail, ornate elements | 1.0 |

### Palette

Urban Kaladesh—warm metallics, aether accents, dense architecture. Per [art_style_guide.md](../../art_style_guide.md).

## Volcano / Lava

**Design intent:** Volcanic terrain from above—**strict overhead view**. Molten lava rivers, volcanic rock formations, glowing magma. **Kaladesh integration**—ancient temple or structure ruins in volcanic terrain. Gilded accents (#B5A642), copper (#B87333). Jewel-like magma crystals. Ornate volcanic structures. Aether blue accents in lava flows. Ancient power; beauty alongside raw nature.

![Volcano theme sample](theme_volcano_sample.png)  
*Overhead view: lava rivers, volcanic rock, temple ruins. Parallax: far volcanic haze, mid lava flows/structures, near rock detail/ornate elements.*

### Parallax Layers

| Layer | Content | Scroll Ratio |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| Far | Distant volcanic haze, crater rims | 0.3 |
| Mid | Lava flows, temple ruins, rock formations | 0.6 |
| Near | Rock detail, magma crystals, ornate elements | 1.0 |

### Palette

#2d2d2d, #3d2914 (volcanic rock), #FF6B35, #FF4500, #FFBF00 (molten orange/amber), #B5A642 (gold), #B87333 (copper), aether blue in lava
