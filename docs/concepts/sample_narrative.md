# Sample Narrative — Opening

**For P0.6 investor pitch material**

---

Long before steel and gunpowder, humanity learned to fly. The Vimanas—ancient chariots of wood and bronze, powered by aether and ingenuity—were our first defense when the swarm came. They came from the sky: insectoid invaders, hive-minded and relentless, descending from a Mother Ship that blotted out the sun. Earth’s chosen pilots climbed into their machines and rose to meet them.

The stakes were clear. Every wave that broke through meant villages burned, families scattered, and the hive grew stronger. Pilots mattered because they were the only ones who could fly the Vimanas—destined citizens, forged by fate and training to hold the line. Alone, a pilot could stall. Together, they could push back. Cooperation wasn’t a tactic; it was survival.

Today, the hangar doors open. The swarm is massing again. Four pilots ready their ships. Their first mission awaits.
