## Terrain Types (Levels 1–3)

### Level 1: Coastline
- **Primary terrain:** Ocean + landmass. Coastline curves from left to right.
- **Parallax layers:** Far (ocean, horizon haze), Mid (coastline, terrain split), Near (wave crests, shoreline detail).
- **Palette:** Deep blue ocean (#1a3a5c), earth brown (#4a3728), copper (#b87333), olive vegetation (#6b8e23).
- **Atmosphere:** Warm metallics in horizon; steampunk. Not abstract sky.
- **Reference:** 1943_ref_1.png, 1943_ref_2.png, single_player_gameplay_mockup.md.

### Level 2: Forest
- **Primary terrain:** Forest canopy from above. Trees, foliage, varied ground.
- **Parallax layers:** Far (distant sky, horizon), Mid (canopy, tree tops), Near (branches, foliage detail).
- **Palette:** Varied greens (#2d6a2d, #6b8e23, #8fbc8f). Earth brown, deep shadows.
- **Atmosphere:** Dense but readable. Depth through shade variation.
- **Reference:** aero_fighters_ref_1.png, sophisticated_ref_3.png, sophisticated_ref_4.png.

### Level 3: Industrial
- **Primary terrain:** Industrial structures, pipes, conduits.
- **Parallax layers:** Far (distant sky, horizon), Mid (pipes, structures), Near (conduits, machinery detail).
- **Palette:** Grey (#4a4a4a), copper, brass. Darker, industrial.
- **Atmosphere:** Dense industrial. See sophisticated_ref_5.png, sophisticated_ref_7.png.

## Wave Framing for Investor Mockup

**Goal:** Show a meaningful mid-level moment. Not the start (empty), not the boss (different). Mid-wave engagement.

### Composition
- **Player:** Sparrow (or representative ship) in lower third. Center or slightly left. "Home" zone.
- **Enemies:** 5–7 small enemy ships (Scouts) in V or staggered wedge. Upper half of screen. Descending.
- **Action:** Player has fired; 1–2 enemies destroyed (explosion, smoke). Others advancing.
- **Projectiles:** Player cyan/white streaks (2–3 visible). Enemy amber/green (1–2 incoming).
- **HUD:** HP, mana, score, lives. Readable. Functional.

### Wave Composition (first wave, early level)
- **Count:** 5–7 Scouts
- **Formation:** V or staggered wedge. Hive-mind, coordinated.
- **Position:** Upper half of screen
- **Behavior read:** Approaching from above; threat direction clear

### Pacing
- **Moment:** Mid-wave. Not overwhelming; not empty. "This is the game."
- **Readability:** Every element reads instantly: friend, foe, play space, threat.

## Mockup Checklist

- [ ] Top-down (bird's eye) perspective throughout
- [ ] 4 parallax layers (far, mid, near, play plane)
- [ ] Terrain split visible (water vs land, or forest vs sky, etc.)
- [ ] Player ship with engine glow, projectiles
- [ ] Enemy wave in formation
- [ ] At least one explosion (enemy destroyed)
- [ ] HUD elements (HP, mana, score, lives)
- [ ] Sophisticated 16-bit: detail, atmosphere, mature aesthetic
