---

## What This Shows

This mockup represents **actual gameplay**—what players see mid-level while playing. It is not a menu, cutscene, or abstract concept. It is the core loop: pilot a steampunk ship, fight waves of insectoid invaders, progress through terrain-based levels.

**Key elements visible:**
- **Player ship (Sparrow):** Steampunk flying machine, cobalt/cyan, bird-inspired. Glowing engine, clear projectiles.
- **Enemy wave:** 5–7 insectoid scouts in V-formation. Amber/olive palette. Angular, alien.
- **Combat:** Mid-wave engagement. Projectiles, explosions, threat direction clear.
- **Level:** Coastline. Ocean, landmass, layered parallax. 1943 / Aero Fighters style.
- **HUD:** HP, mana, score, lives. Functional, readable.

---

## Visual Pillars

1. **Sophisticated 2D:** High-fidelity, detailed sprites. Metallic textures, glowing effects. Mature aesthetic—not childish.
2. **Steampunk meets ancient civilization:** Brass, copper, flying machines. Earth defenders vs alien invaders.
3. **Top-down clarity:** Every element reads instantly. Friend, foe, threat, play space.
4. **Terrain-based levels:** Ocean, forest, mountains, industrial. Not abstract sky.
5. **Cooperative ready:** 1–4 players. Same visual language scales to multiplayer.
