# MILESTONE_001: Ship Design — First Deliverable

**Director · 2025-03-01 · Recalibrated**

## Intent

**Ships only.** Design the four player ships (Sparrow, Turtle, Wolf, Dragon). Ships are the heart of the game—what players control. Iterate purely on ship concepts until approved.

Pilots, levels, enemies, and investor mockup are **deferred** to later milestones.

---

## Theme Correction

**This is not a steampunk game.** Primary theme: **early civilization + ancient aliens (Stargate-type)**. Ancient flying machines (Vimanas) from mythology; technology that feels alien-derived or inspired. Steampunk can be an **influence** (brass, copper, mechanical detail) but not the primary aesthetic.

---

## Primary Concern: Top-Down View

The top-down (bird's eye) view is what we're most concerned with. Ships are seen from above during gameplay. Silhouette, facing, and readability at that angle are paramount.
